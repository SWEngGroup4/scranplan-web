Scranplans Features

The Basics

Scranplan has a large database of recipes and the ability to create your own recipes with customization over each individual step in a recipe.

Mealplanner

Plan the recipes for the week with the easy to use mealplanner and export your mealplan as a shopping list when your done.

Made to be shared

Share recipes and images on your feed with friends and rate recipes from others.
Gain Kudos and grow as a chef as you earn rewards.

Unique customization

With a wide array of customization options from changing the layout and styling of the text to adding timers,, images, videos and comments to individual steps make scranplan your own and never look back.

Expiry dates & Portion control

Check when recipes are going out of date and scale recipes ingredients portions using Scranplans averaging algorithm for those times when you need to feed the five armys.

Advanced privacy settings

Have full customization over who can see your profile with private/public access and the ability to hide most if not all content on your profile if required.

Allergens and dietary preferences

Provide filters for certain ingredients to make sure recipes fit your preferences and your not left with the yellow pages equivalent of a cooking app.

Advanced searching using Algolia

Can you only remember a couple of ingredients and not the entire name of a recipe. Don't worry scranplans advanced searching using Algolia has you covered.
