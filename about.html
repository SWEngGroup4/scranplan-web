Scran Plan | Overview

The Basics

Scranplan has a large database of recipes and the ability to create your own recipes with customization over each individual step in a recipe.

Mealplanner

Plan the recipes for the week with the easy to use mealplanner and export your mealplan as a shopping list when your done.

Made to be shared

Share recipes and images on your feed with friends and rate recipes from others.
Gain Kudos and grow as a chef as you earn rewards.

Unique customization

With a wide array of customization options from changing the layout and styling of the text to adding timers,, images, videos and comments to individual steps make scranplan your own and never look back.

Expiry dates & Portion control

Check when recipes are going out of date and scale recipes ingredients portions using Scranplans averaging algorithm for those times when you need to feed the five armys.

Advanced privacy settings

Have full customization over who can see your profile with private/public access and the ability to hide most if not all content on your profile if required.

Allergens and dietary preferences

Provide filters for certain ingredients to make sure recipes fit your preferences and your not left with the yellow pages equivalent of a cooking app.

Advanced searching using Algolia

Can you only remember a couple of ingredients and not the entire name of a recipe. Don't worry scranplans advanced searching using Algolia has you covered.

Home Screen | Recipe & Recipe Creation

After launching into the app, you are greated with the home screen this is where all the recipes are located and allows you to get straight in with making a recipe. As you can see adverts are intergrated directly in the feed. After clicking on a recipe you are taken to the recipe infomation screen which has the ingredients and the reviews associated with the recipe. Clicking Let's Cook launches into the presentation.

To create a recipe it is as simple as clicking the plus button on the lower third of the screen and selecting create recipe. This allows you to directly create an XML presentation. You simply follow the instructions filling in relevent information together with alergens and additional infomation for the recipe.

Once entering the recipe details, it is possible to create steps to make the recipe. The recipe creation is entirely customizable and can be presented in a way unique for every user with graphics, audio and media options for each step. Like a scrapbook, users can make it their own and this in turn is helpful in distinguishing us from other competitors who don’t have these customisation options. 

Home Screen | Searching

Searching is another major component of the app built using the Algoila framework, it allows us to search using multiple filters and to search for recipes and users in the app. We currently have a wide selection of recipes available to users and this range will increase over time - with users adding their own recipes and getting more companies on board to provide recipes. We also have recipes in different categories, to allow easier browsing and quicker results.

After searching for recipes/users it is possible to launch into the profile of the user or onto the recipe and get cooking straight away.

Meal Planner

The Meal Planner allows users to plan meals for the week, the first image shows the welcome screen to the meal planner and the second and third are typical meal plans for the week.
The ability to create a meal plan and shopping list isn’t the only bonus.

The shopping list allows the user to create a list of all the ingredients and allow them to delete any they already have in the pantry. This can be saved and viewed at any time. Users also have the ability to change the portion size of the recipe to suit their needs and view the reheating information for batch cooking. Giving users the ability to plan ahead, saving money and time. The recipe infomation screen is also different having reheat and portion control.

Social | User Profiles

Users are able to create fully customisable profiles with the setting for private profiles or public profiles. This allows them to control what people get to see about them and their presence in the app.

Images 1 & 2 show the steps taken to follow a private profile. Image 3 is a public profile being followed. Each profile building up its own chef rank, built on Kudos earned as created recipes are shared and enjoyed. With 3 seperate pages for all posts, photos and recipes all with links to recipes on the app users can find new recipes and inspiration with a simple click.

Scran Plan allows users to follow anyone! Explore the best recipes by world renowned chefs or up and coming amateurs finding their flavour. These images highlight the notfication structure when a user requests to follow a private profile.

Social | Feed and Messaging

The feed allows users to interact with each other, write review, link recipes and share tips and tricks about cooking. The feed makes our app unique and puts us apart from other applications, we allow users to share review, images and recipes to their following together with having the facility for direct messsging.

It is also possible to share recipes more personally with direct messaging built in the app. It is possible to directly message any user you are following and get in realtime replies.  The messenger is accessed from the side menu and clicking the new messages button on the lower right allows users to create new messges to users who they are following.

Gold Membership and User Feedback

We accept user feedback from our suggestions box in the side bar and also allow users to report content if they feel that it's inapproprate. Gold membership can be purchased from the sidebar (although not operational in this release)
